Bobm Tag

Bobm Tag was my first complete gameplay-focused project. It introduced pressure-driven mechanics and timed state transitions.

Design limitations at the time:
  • Minimal player feedback and UI
  • Limited understanding of player psychology
  • Basic balancing and difficulty curves
  • Naive handling of edge cases

Many later systems evolved from these early experiments.

Early mechanics still influence current projects.
